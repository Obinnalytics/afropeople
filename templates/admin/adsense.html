AdSense Integration Guide
How to Get AdSense Codes:
  1. Sign up for Google AdSense
  2. Get your site approved by Google
  3. Create ad units in your AdSense dashboard
  4. Copy the ad codes and paste them below
  5. Enable AdSense to start showing ads
Ad Placement Locations:
  • Header: Top of every page
  • Sidebar: Right side of content pages
  • Footer: Bottom of every page
  • Post Content: Within post content area
